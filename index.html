Kara Tour – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2025

1. Overview

Kara Tour is a simple tourism information app that provides details about hotels, tourist destinations, and tour prices in Uzbekistan. The app is designed to help users explore and learn about places of interest.

2. No Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There is no login, account creation, analytics, or tracking of user behavior.

3. Content and Sources

All content in the app is informational and provided in the Uzbek language. The app includes images and descriptions of locations. Much of the information is based on publicly available content from Wikipedia, which has been translated for local understanding. Proper references are provided where applicable.

4. Third-Party Services

Kara Tour uses Google Maps API to display maps and preview the locations of tourist destinations. Google Maps may collect data such as your IP address, location (if you allow it), and device information, in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

Kara Tour does not access or store this data. All map-related features are powered and managed by Google.

5. Security

Since Kara Tour does not collect personal data, there is no user data stored or processed within the app. However, external services like Google Maps may handle data securely according to their own terms and policies.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy if the app changes in the future. All changes will be posted on this page. However, as of now, the app remains a simple, offline informational tool.
